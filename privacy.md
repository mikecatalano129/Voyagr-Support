# Voyagr Social Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026

Voyagr Social (“Voyagr,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is a social platform that helps cruise passengers connect with others on the same sailing.

## Information We Collect

We may collect:

- Account information, including your name, email address, password credentials, and user ID.
- Profile information, including your profile photo, general hometown or location, and biography.
- Sailing information, including the sailing you join and related membership information.
- User content, including posts, photos, comments, events, RSVPs, messages, and reports.
- App activity, including likes and other interactions with Voyagr features.

Passwords are handled through our authentication provider and are not available to other Voyagr users.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Create and authenticate accounts.
- Connect passengers on the same sailing.
- Display profiles, posts, comments, events, and RSVPs.
- Provide private messaging.
- Operate safety, moderation, reporting, and account-management features.
- Maintain, secure, troubleshoot, and improve Voyagr.

Voyagr does not sell personal information or use personal information for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

## Sharing and Service Providers

Information you choose to post or include in your profile may be visible to other members of your sailing.

Voyagr uses Supabase for authentication, database, and file-storage services. Service providers may process information only to provide services to Voyagr and are expected to protect it appropriately.

We may disclose information when required by law or when reasonably necessary to protect users, enforce our policies, prevent fraud, or address safety concerns.

## Data Retention and Deletion

We retain information while your account is active and as reasonably necessary to operate Voyagr, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and meet legal obligations.

You can delete your account from the Profile tab inside the Voyagr app. Account deletion is intended to remove the account and associated user content, subject to any information we must retain to comply with applicable law.

You may also request assistance by emailing mikecatalano129@gmail.com.

## Your Choices

You can edit profile information, change your password, leave a sailing, report content or users, and delete your account through the app.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards designed to protect information. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

## Children

Voyagr is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Voyagr develops. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
